Tanjung website

Located 3 hours drive from Jakarta, Tanjung Lesung is a special economic zone that is being developed by Jababeka to become an international tourist destination. In an area of 1500 hectares which will later be accessible via toll roads, airports, and the sea, many new rides will be built such as amusement parks, marinas, etc. Currently, there are hotels, beach clubs, diving centers, Mongolian cultural centers and golf courses built by Jababeka. Tanjung Lesung is also the entry point for tourists visiting Ujung Kulon National Park and Krakatau Island.
